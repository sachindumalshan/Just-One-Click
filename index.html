What is JOC ?

JOC is open to those who are enthusiastic about the techniques of photography, and videography and improving them further.

  • Just One Click (JOC) is an initiative by AIESEC in

    Uva Wellassa University, mainly focusing on individuals who are enthusiastic about photography.

  • The participants will get the opportunity

    to excel in their knowledge of photography along with that, they are able to enter a competition and win valuable gifts.


Who organize JOC?

" JOC ", organized by the AIESEC in Uva Wellassa University is ready to offer you an outstanding opportunity to enhance your knowledge in photography and videography through an awareness session organized with the cooperation of the experts in the field.
